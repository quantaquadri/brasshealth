Join Brass

Whatever your health goals are, we’ll help you build a science-based workout plan tailored to your needs.

Brass offers month-to-month standard memberships for workouts. We have classes throughout the day, 6 days a week.


We recommend 2 classes per week at a minimum.



Included in your plan


1. Brass Strength Bands and water bottle

2. Weekly small-group, live virtual training classes

3. Initial fitness assessment to set baselines

4. Follow-up assessments every 8 weeks*

5. Nutrition analysis with a registered dietitian
